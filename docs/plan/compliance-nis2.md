NIS2 Directive (EU 2022/2555) establishes unified cybersecurity framework for 18 critical sectors.

Full compliance documentation including requirements, audit trails, gap analysis, and implementation roadmap will be maintained in this document.

---

## Overview

NIS2 Directive expands the scope of the original NIS Directive to cover more sectors and organizations, with stricter requirements for risk management, incident reporting, and supply chain security.

---

## Key Requirements

- Risk management measures and cybersecurity policies
- Incident reporting to national CSIRTs within 24-72 hours
- Supply chain security assessments
- Encryption and multi-factor authentication
- Regular cybersecurity training for staff
- Vulnerability disclosure processes

---

## Sector Coverage

The directive covers 18 critical sectors including energy, transport, banking, health, digital infrastructure, and more.
